Let me introduce you to the concept of Proprietary Trading Firms (Prop Firms).

What is a Prop Firm? Isipin mo sila bilang mga "Silent Investors." These are large companies with Millions of Dollars in their vault.

  • The Problem: They have the money, but they don't have the time to trade it.
  • The Solution: They are looking for "Fund Managers" (pwedeng ikaw 'yun!) para palaguin ang pera nila.

Hindi ito utang (Loan). Hindi mo kailangang magsangla ng lupa o magpakita ng payslip. Prop Firms don't care about your credit score. They only care about one thing: SKILL.

The Deal: Skill for Capital Ganito ang proseso. It is a fair trade:

  1. The Challenge (Ang Audition): Bago ka nila bigyan ng pera, kailangan mong patunayan na hindi ka "sugalero." You have to pass an evaluation phase.
    • Target: Hit a specific profit goal (usually 8-10%).
    • Rule: Do not lose too much money (Risk Management).
  2. The Funded Account (Ang Pondo): Kapag pumasa ka, ibibigay nila sa'yo ang access sa Live Capital. Pwede kang mag-handle ng account mula $10,000 hanggang $100,000.
  3. The Profit Split (Ang Hati): Ito ang pinakamaganda. Kapag kumita ka gamit ang pera nila, you keep up to 80% of the profit. Sila ang kukuha ng 20%.
    • Example: If you make $5,000 profit using their money, $4,000 is YOURS.

Why is this the perfect business model? Because the risk is on them. Kapag natalo ka sa trade (basta within the rules), hindi mo kailangang bayaran ang talo. Pera nila 'yun. Pero kapag nanalo ka? Bulsa mo ang majority ng kita.

This is how you start a business with Zero Financial Risk.
